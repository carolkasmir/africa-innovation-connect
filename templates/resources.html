Funding Opportunities

  • African Development Bank (AfDB)

    Provides financial and technical assistance for projects that promote economic development and social progress in African countries.

  • Anzisha Prize

    Offers funding and support for young African entrepreneurs aged 15-22 who have innovative business ideas and are making an impact in their communities.

  • The African Innovation Foundation

    Provides grants and support for innovative projects and initiatives that have the potential to transform African communities.

  • MEST Africa

    Offers funding and training for aspiring tech entrepreneurs across Africa. MEST supports businesses through mentorship, investment, and resources.

Business Development Resources

  • African Women in Tech (AWIT)

    A platform that empowers women in technology through mentorship, training, and networking opportunities.

  • Startupbootcamp Africa

    Provides an accelerator program that supports startups with mentorship, access to funding, and global exposure.

  • The African Entrepreneurship Collective

    Supports African entrepreneurs through training, mentorship, and access to capital to grow their businesses.

Educational Resources

  • Coursera for Africa

    Offers online courses from top universities worldwide for African learners, helping them gain skills in various fields including entrepreneurship.

  • Google Digital Skills for Africa

    Provides free training on digital marketing, entrepreneurship, and various digital skills to help Africans succeed in a digital economy.

  • The African Leadership Academy

    A pan-African institution that develops young leaders through education, mentorship, and networking opportunities.
